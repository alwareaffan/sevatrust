About Seva Charitable Trust

Seva Charitable Trust is a not for profit organization committed to serving underprivileged families and building stronger, healthier communities. Through targeted programs in women empowerment, education, and health care, Seva works hand in hand with communities to create lasting positive change.

Mission

To empower vulnerable communities through education, health care, and sustainable livelihood opportunities while nurturing equality and dignity for every person.

Vision

A world where every individual has access to basic needs and the opportunity to thrive.
