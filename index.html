Serving Humanity with Compassion

Seva Charitable Trust is a registered non governmental organization dedicated to social welfare, education support, women empowerment, medical assistance and community upliftment.

About Seva Charitable Trust

Our Background

Seva Charitable Trust was established in the year 2024 and is registered under the Mumbai Public Trust Act, 1950. The trust is committed to serving society with honesty, transparency and compassion.

Our Mission

To uplift underprivileged communities by providing social support, education assistance, healthcare aid and empowerment opportunities with dignity and care.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual has access to basic needs, education and opportunities for growth.

Our Work & Initiatives

Education support for underprivileged children
Women empowerment and skill development programs
Medical aid and healthcare assistance
Community welfare and social awareness initiatives
